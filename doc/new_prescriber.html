Adding a new prescriber

To create a new prescriber go to the Prescribers tab. Click on New Prescriber.

Fill in the last and first name. The Full name is what appears on a prescription bottle indicating who wrote the prescription.

Modifying an existing prescriber

TO modify an existing prescriber go to the Prescribers tab. Search for the prescriber you wish to change and then right-click the entry. Select Modify Prescriber. From here on the process is the same as adding a new prescriber.
