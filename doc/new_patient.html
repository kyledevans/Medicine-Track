Adding a new patient

To create a new patient go to the Patients tab. Once there it's a good idea to first search for the patient to avoid duplicate patient entries.

Click on New Patient to bring up the New Patient Wizard. The M.R. Number is the patient's ID from Allscripts EMR. When all the fields have been entered, click Finish.

Modifying an existing patient

To modify an existing patient go to the Patients tab. Search for the patient you wish to modify. Right-click them and select Modify Patient. The process is the same from here on as adding a new patient.
