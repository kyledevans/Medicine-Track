Adding new inventory

To enter new inventory go to the Formulary tab. Search for and select the medication you need. You may either right click the medication and select Add stock or click the button just above medication list.

Enter the details for the new inventory. All fields are mandatory. A print dialog will pop up once you have finished the wizard allowing you to print a barcode.
