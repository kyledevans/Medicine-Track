Search inventory

To search for inventory go to the Inventory tab. From there you can search based on the medication name or lot number. You can also search for a specific entry by scanning a barcode.

  • Uncheck In Stock to include inventory that is no longer in stock.
  • Uncheck Active to only show inventory that is no longer active.
  • Uncheck Not Expired to include inventory that is expired.
